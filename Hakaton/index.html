Dive into the underwater world of Israel

Israel has a rich diving culture that might surprise you because it's not just about the beautiful reef of Eilat. Archaeological and historical wonders await in Israel's warm waters, as well as animal species you may not associate with the Middle East - dolphins, octopuses, sea urchins, and even a few sea turtles, whose numbers have increased thanks to organizations such as Israel's Sea Turtle Rescue Center .

Endless variety of flora and fauna will make your dive unforgettable!
